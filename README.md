Privacy Policy
My Equity Calculator (Android app, package com.ciy.myequitycalculator)
Effective date: July 2, 2026 · Last updated: July 2, 2026

Summary: My Equity Calculator is private by design. It requires no account, no sign-in, and no bank or brokerage linking. Your financial entries (stocks, retirement holdings, properties) are stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal or financial data.

1. Who we are
My Equity Calculator is developed and published by CIY ("we", "us"). Contact: chad.yeo.cy@gmail.com.

2. Data you enter stays on your device
Everything you enter in the app — stock holdings, quantities, cost basis, notes, retirement accounts and balances, property details (including any optional address or nickname), and projection assumptions — is stored in a local database on your device only. This information:

is never uploaded to our servers (we operate none),
is never shared with third parties,
is never used for advertising or analytics,
can be deleted at any time by removing entries in the app or uninstalling the app.
If you use Android's system backup (Google-managed device backup), your device may include the app's local data in your personal device backup under Google's terms. You can exclude the app from backups in Android settings.

3. Market price lookups
To show current prices, the app requests quotes for the ticker symbols you track (for example, "AAPL" or "VTSAX") from a third-party market data service (currently Yahoo Finance endpoints). These requests:

contain only the ticker symbol being priced — never your name, holdings, quantities, balances, or any account identifier;
necessarily expose standard network information to that service (such as your IP address and a browser-style user agent), as with any internet request;
are made only while you use the app (on open, pull-to-refresh, or when you add a holding) — there is no background polling.
We do not control the third-party service's processing of standard network metadata. Market data is provided for informational purposes only and may be delayed.

4. Data we collect: none
The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no crash-reporting SDKs. We do not collect:

personal information (name, email, contacts, location),
financial account credentials (we never ask for them),
device identifiers for tracking,
usage analytics.
If a future version adds any such SDK, this policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated before release.

5. Permissions the app uses
INTERNET — to fetch market prices for tickers you track.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect whether a connection is available so the app can fall back to cached prices offline.
The app requests no other permissions — no location, camera, contacts, storage, or microphone access.

6. Data retention and deletion
Because your data lives only on your device, you are in full control:

Delete individual entries with a swipe inside the app.
Delete everything by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in Android settings.
We have no copy of your data and therefore cannot access, restore, or delete it on your behalf.

7. Security
Your entries are stored in the app's private, sandboxed storage area, which Android isolates from other apps. Price requests are made over HTTPS.

8. Children
My Equity Calculator is a finance tool intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children (or, as described above, from anyone).

9. Not financial advice
Projections shown in the app are hypothetical estimates based on assumptions you enter and current market prices. The app does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice.

10. Changes to this policy
If we change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Material changes (such as adding an ads or analytics SDK) will also be reflected in the app's Google Play Data Safety section before the updated app ships.
